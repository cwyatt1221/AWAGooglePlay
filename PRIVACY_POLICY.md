# Privacy Policy — Animal Wellness Action App

*Publish this as a page on animalwellnessaction.org and link that URL in the
Google Play Console listing. Google requires a live, publicly reachable URL —
this file itself won't satisfy that requirement.*

**Last updated: [DATE]**

The Animal Wellness Action mobile app displays content from
animalwellnessaction.org and periodically downloads a copy of certain pages
so they remain viewable when your device is offline.

**Information we collect:** The app itself does not collect, store, or share
any personal information. Standard network information (such as your device's
IP address) is transmitted as part of loading web pages, the same as it would
be in a mobile browser.

**Offline storage:** A daily copy of select public pages (e.g., the homepage,
Congressional Profiles, and Endorsements pages) is stored locally on your
device to allow offline viewing. This data is not transmitted anywhere and is
deleted if you uninstall the app.

**Third-party content:** Pages loaded in the app may include content or
scripts from the same third parties used on animalwellnessaction.org (for
example, analytics or embedded media). Refer to
[animalwellnessaction.org's website privacy policy] for details on those
services.
